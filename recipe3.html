EASY-MADE PANCAKES

Posted Jul 3

Ingredients SERVINGS: 1 medium carrot (peeled, finely shredded with a mandoline) 1 beet (medium cooked, finely shredded with a mandoline) 1/2 white radish (large, finely shredded with a mandoline) lemon juice (for drizzling) extra-virgin olive oil (for drizzling) ground cumin (for sprinkling) lemon wedges (for garnish)

Arrange shredded vegetables in piles on a serving platter. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil. Sprinkle lightly with ground cumin. Garnish with lemon and orange wedges.
